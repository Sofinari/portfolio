About me

I am deeply fascinated by the dynamic evolution of communication through various media, particularly in the area of digital platforms. My path began with a training as an Interactive Media Designer at CSIA, followed by a Bachelor's degree in Visual Communication at SUPSI University in Mendrisio, Switzerland.

Driven by a passion for innovation and creativity, I am committed to exploring the intersection of design and technology. My career has been enriched by practical experiences, including working with pixel-plus , where I contributed to several projects that required technical UX/UI skills.

My next aspiration is to pursue a master's degree specializing in video game creation, with the goal of further deepening my understanding of interactive media and narrative experiences.
